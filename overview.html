Overview

My current research interests include next-generation network (cloud, mobile edge computing, fog computing, etc.) control, especially in the intersection of 3C (computing, caching, and communication), stochastic network control and optimization. I also built expertise on multi-agent (cooperative) network localization, navigation and control from my previous research.
